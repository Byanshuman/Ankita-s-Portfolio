About Me

I am Dr. Ankita Sagar, specializing in Agricultural Biochemistry with research experience in pulses, rice, mango kernel utilization, crop biochemistry, and food quality. I have worked as Senior Research Fellow at the Indian Institute of Pulses Research and currently serve as Assistant Professor and Faculty Coordinator at United University, Prayagraj.

Total Experience: 7 Years 5 Months
